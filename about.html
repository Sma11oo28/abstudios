Our Story

5+ Years of digital competence

Some stories begin in the most unexpected places. For AB Studios, it all started in the back row of a high school classroom, where two friends—Ryan (CEO) and Lawrence (Creative Director)—bonded over mischief, creativity, and an unshakable curiosity for innovation.

Ryan, with no natural talent for art, often relied on Lawrence's artistic eye for school projects. Even when a class reshuffle separated them, their friendship remained strong. Years later, after pursuing separate paths—Ryan in Computer Science and Lawrence in Graphic Design—their shared passion for problem-solving brought them back together.

Over a casual meet-up, with Lawrence savoring his tea and cake while Ryan enjoyed a refreshing glass of passion juice, an idea took shape: to merge creativity and technology into something bigger. They envisioned a company that would help businesses stand out in the digital space, combining the power of design, storytelling, and cutting-edge tech.

That idea became AB Studios—a digital solutions powerhouse committed to pushing creative boundaries and empowering brands with innovative, disruptive, and strategic digital experiences. From building that very first website to now serving a diverse range of clients, our journey has been one of continuous learning, evolution, and impact.

At AB Studios, we don’t just create; we transform ideas into digital excellence.
